Kate Bush

English singer/songwriter

Kate Bush had an early introduction to the artistic life

  • Kate Bush was born on July 30, 1958, Bexleyheath, Kent, England.
  • Her parents were medical professionals who enjoyed music and frequently performed English and Irish tunes at home with Kate and her older brothers.
  • Kate's doctor father played the piano and her nurse mother competed as a folk dancer in her native Ireland.
  • At the age of sixteen, a family friend introduced Kate to Pink Floyd guitarist, David Gilmour, who helped her win a contract with EMI records.
  • Kate's debut single, "Wuthering Heights" was released a few years later and become an instant number-one hit.
